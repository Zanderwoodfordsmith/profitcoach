What Is This
This shows you how to edit the message templates to make them effective for your ideal.

The personalisation happens in the connection message and message 1 (and a word or two change in the final message 5). So we have templates for this. All the templates use a very similar structure but you may have to adapt part of it based on your credibility and experience as a coach:
Client proof - this is where you use the proof of people you have helped in the past. It is the best form of proof out of these 3.
Career Proof - this is where you use proof from things you have achieved in your career to build credibility.
You only need to do one of these!
