# Cashflow Forecaster

## What This Is

A training video and worksheet that teaches business owners how to build and use a 13-week cash flow forecast. This is the foundation of the entire Profit & Cash Flow area — understanding where your cash is going before it disappears.

## Short Video Script — How to Use the Cash Flow Forecaster

**Target length:** 2–3 minutes
**Purpose:** Quick walkthrough so a business owner can open the worksheet and get started immediately

---

**[INTRO — 15 seconds]**

"If you don't know where your cash is going over the next 13 weeks, you're flying blind. This tool fixes that. I'm going to show you exactly how to use the Cash Flow Forecaster — it'll take you about 15 minutes to set up, and then 5 minutes a week to keep it running."

**[STEP 1: OPEN THE WORKSHEET — 20 seconds]**

"Open the Cash Flow Forecaster worksheet. You'll see 13 columns — one for each week — and rows split into two sections: cash coming in, and cash going out. Don't overcomplicate this. We just need the real numbers."

**[STEP 2: FILL IN YOUR CASH IN — 30 seconds]**

"Start with cash in. Go week by week and enter what you're expecting to receive. Invoices you've already sent, recurring revenue, any confirmed payments. If you're not sure, put in your best estimate — a rough number is infinitely better than a blank cell. The goal is to see what's actually landing in your account each week."

**[STEP 3: FILL IN YOUR CASH OUT — 30 seconds]**

"Now do cash out. Rent, wages, subscriptions, supplier payments, tax — anything leaving your account. Start with the fixed costs you know, then add the variable ones. If a big expense is coming up — VAT, equipment, a quarterly bill — put it in the week it's due. No surprises."

**[STEP 4: SEE YOUR POSITION — 20 seconds]**

"The bottom row shows your running cash position — what's left after everything in and everything out. This is the number that matters. If it goes negative in week 6, you know now, not in week 6. That's the whole point."

**[STEP 5: THE WEEKLY REVIEW — 20 seconds]**

"Every Friday, spend 5 minutes updating this. Move your actuals in, adjust your forecasts, and roll the window forward one week. You always want to see 13 weeks ahead. That's it — 5 minutes and you'll never be caught off guard by a cash crunch again."

**[CLOSE — 15 seconds]**

"This is the single most important financial habit you can build. It turns panic into planning. Open the worksheet, fill it in today, and review it every Friday. You'll wonder how you ever ran your business without it."
