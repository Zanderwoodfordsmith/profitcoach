# Profit First Philosophy & Implementation

## What This Is

A training resource covering the Profit First philosophy — Mike Michalowicz's system of allocating revenue into separate accounts (profit, owner's pay, tax, operating expenses) before spending. Includes the implementation spreadsheet and a YouTube video for deeper learning.
