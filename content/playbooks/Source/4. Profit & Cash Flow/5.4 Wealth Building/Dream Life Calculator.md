# Dream Life Calculator

## What This Is

A calculator tool that helps business owners work backwards from their ideal lifestyle to determine exactly how much their business needs to generate. Turns vague ideas about financial freedom into a specific, concrete number — which then becomes the foundation for their wealth building goals.

## Playbook Connection

**Supports:** [[5.4 Wealth Building]]
**Play:** Defining the wealth target — what does financial freedom actually cost?

## Notes

This is a powerful session tool. Most clients have never actually calculated what their dream life costs — they just know they want "more." Running this calculator in session often creates a meaningful moment of clarity. Use it at Level 5 when the client is ready to think beyond the business and into personal wealth and legacy. Connects directly to [[5.0 Life Design]] for designing the life this wealth supports, and [[5.7 Business Valuation]] for understanding the business's role in the wealth equation.
