## **What This Is**

Lead generation is the lifeblood of growth. Without a consistent flow of qualified prospects, every month feels like starting from zero. You win some deals, celebrate briefly, then wake up next month with an empty pipeline and have to scramble again.

This playbook builds a systematic acquisition engine so you never have to pray that the phone rings. It covers the full lead generation system: choosing the right channels, creating content that attracts prospects, capturing them effectively, and measuring what actually works. The goal is predictable, consistent lead flow that feeds your sales process every week.

This is not about being everywhere at once. It is about finding what works for your business and doubling down on it.

---

## **What It Looks Like**

### **🔴 When this is broken**

You never know where next month's leads will come from. Most of your business comes from word of mouth or one-off marketing efforts that worked once but you cannot replicate. You try random tactics — social media one week, paid ads the next — but nothing sticks. You spend money on marketing but cannot tell if it is working because you do not track the numbers.

### **🟠 When this is OK**

You get some leads from a couple of channels but the flow is inconsistent. Some months are good, others are terrible, and you are not sure what made the difference. You know roughly what you spend on marketing but not what it costs to get each lead. You have content but it feels random rather than strategic.

### **🟢 When this is working**

You have 2-3 lead generation channels that consistently produce qualified prospects every week. You know exactly what it costs to generate a lead from each channel and which channels deliver the highest quality prospects. Your content strategy is focused and builds authority in your niche. You have weekly lead targets and you hit them regularly through a mix of organic and paid efforts.

---

## **Things to Think About**

Which channel consistently produces your best customers? Most businesses spread their effort across five channels when one or two could deliver all the leads they need. Double down on what is already working before chasing the next shiny marketing tactic.

Do you know what it actually costs to get a qualified lead? Without cost-per-lead numbers, you are flying blind. You might be spending £100 to generate a lead worth £50, or missing out on a channel that delivers £500 leads for £20 each.

Is your content strategy focused or scattered? Publishing random content hoping something sticks is not a strategy. The best lead generation content consistently addresses the same problems for the same people. Repetition builds authority.

Are you capturing leads or just creating awareness? Getting attention is not the same as generating leads. Every piece of marketing should have a clear next step that moves prospects from "interested" to "contact details captured."

What weekly volume do you need to hit your revenue targets? If you need £50k revenue and your average deal is £5k, you need ten customers. If 20% of leads convert, you need fifty leads. Work backwards from revenue to weekly lead targets.

## **The Plays Inside This Playbook**

This playbook is organised into three sections that build your complete lead generation system:

### **Channel Strategy**

Finding the 2-3 channels that work best for your business.

Play 1: Channel Audit — Review all the ways leads currently find you, identify what is working and what is not, and eliminate the channels that drain time without delivering results.

Play 2: Channel Selection — Choose 2-3 primary channels to focus on based on where your ideal customers actually spend time and where you can realistically compete.

Play 3: Channel Testing Plan — Set up simple tests for each channel so you can measure what works and scale up the winners while killing the losers quickly.

### **Content & Messaging**

Creating content that attracts prospects and positions you as the obvious choice.

Play 4: Content Strategy — Develop a focused content plan that consistently addresses your ideal customer's problems and builds authority in your niche.

Play 5: Lead Magnets — Create valuable resources that prospects exchange their contact details for — the bridge between attention and captured leads.

Play 6: Referral System — Build a simple system to generate referrals from existing customers, the highest-quality lead source most businesses completely ignore.

### **Measurement & Optimisation**

Tracking what matters and improving results over time.

Play 7: Lead Tracking System — Set up simple tracking so you know exactly how many leads each channel generates, what they cost, and which ones convert best.

Play 8: Weekly Lead Targets — Calculate how many leads you need each week to hit your revenue goals and track progress against those targets.

Play 9: Conversion Rate Optimisation — Test and improve how many visitors become leads by optimising landing pages, forms, and calls-to-action.

## **Quick Wins**

This week: Audit your current lead sources. List every way leads found you in the last three months. Circle the top two sources by both volume and quality.

This week: Calculate your target weekly lead volume. Take your monthly revenue goal, divide by average deal size, then multiply by the leads needed per sale. That is your weekly target.

This week: Pick one piece of content you have created recently and turn it into a lead magnet by adding a clear call-to-action and a simple way to capture contact details.
