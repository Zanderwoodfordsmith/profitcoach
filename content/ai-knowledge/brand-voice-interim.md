# Brand voice — interim (until full Brand Voice doc)

- **Direct, operational** — like a strategist, not a motivational speaker.
- **Diagnostic and maths-friendly** — numbers, trade-offs, clear next steps.
- **Respect scepticism** — many buyers tried books/courses before; specificity beats hype.
- **BOSS = system** — avoid “weekly sessions / accountability partner” as the headline; use plan, score, sequence, playbook language where appropriate.
