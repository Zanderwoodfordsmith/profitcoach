# ICP — marketing slice only (tier-2)

**Their language (mirror sparingly):** overwhelmed, doing everything, cash tight, margins shrinking, “what got us here isn’t working,” employees hardest part, lonely, family gets worst version.

**Resonates:** Your score / gap in £, specificity (areas, playbooks), peer-like owners, bounded time, Owner Performance as differentiator.

**Weak:** beach lifestyle fantasy, generic tips, founder credential walls before value, countdown pressure, “transform” without specifics.

**Triggers:** frustration (primary), fear of stagnation, time resentment; hope via **curiosity about the score** and **control** (dashboard/plan).
