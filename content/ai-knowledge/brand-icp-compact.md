# ICP compact — BOSS buyer

- **Who:** Owners £200k–£1M+ wanting ~2–3x; service businesses with teams; UK-first. Not early dreamers, not corporate employees, not “coach me” buyers.
- **Decision:** **Mathematical** (score, gap, ROI), not identity like BCA. They want tools and a plan, not therapy framing.
- **Avoid:** Lifestyle hype, vague transformation promises, aggressive fake urgency, pure “coaching relationship” language. Use operational, diagnostic tone.
