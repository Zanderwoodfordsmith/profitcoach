## The Path From £0 to £10K/mo

By reverse engineering how you produce cashflow, it shows why these 3 pillars are key:

![Reverse engineering cashflow](https://cdn.disco.co/media/CleanShot_2024-11-15_at_17_57_12_2x_b059f0bc-dd6b-4d27-8fce-1a48224dd7dc.png)

We can break down cashflow into the only 2 elements to improve it, as shown above:

* **Get More Customers:** Increase the number of people who buy from you.
* **Make Each Customer Worth More:** Enhance the value each customer brings.

But we don't have to stop there. The 3 building blocks of those two things are:

* **Marketing** — running **campaigns** to generate interested leads.
* **Sales** — taking a lead through **conversion** steps to become a client.
* **Delivery** — giving exceptional **coaching** to produce results and keep clients longer.

Bring it all together and you get the core parts for the **value chain of the business**. The foundational elements:

* **Pillar #1 — Campaigns:** Choosing the right avatar, optimising profiles and running lead generation campaigns.
* **Pillar #2 — Conversion:** Optimising lead follow-up, lead nurture, and converting sales.
* **Pillar #3 — Coaching:** Delivering transformation sessions, developing your skills and following a system.
* **Core Offer:** Setting the right promise, packages and price for your services. (Core offer is part of all three pillars.)

**Key Insight:** If you ever don't have the cashflow you want, it's because you either:

1. Don't have enough customers, or
2. They are not worth enough.

So you need to fix either the marketing, sales or delivery — which ultimately leaves you needing to fix one of these four things: Campaigns, Conversion, Coaching or Core Offer.

### **Pillar #1: Campaigns**

You have to make your offer known to people through campaigns:

* You need to know who and which businesses are the right traffic to generate.
* You need to optimise your main online profile where you will be running traffic.
* And then you need to know the traffic strategies to generate leads.

### **Pillar #2: Conversion**

The higher your conversion, the less work you need for the same results:

* Once you have traffic coming in, you need to properly follow up to get short-term conversion.
* Not everyone will convert, so you need to be able to continuously nurture to increase long-term conversion.
* It's vital you can convert prospects into paying clients through an efficient sales call process.

### **Pillar #3: Coaching**

The better coach you are, the easier it is to make money:

* First you need the coaching skills to understand how to help any business owner, in any situation.
* Then you need to be able to deliver high-value sessions to your clients for quick wins.
* You need to be skilful at creating transformation so you can retain clients.
